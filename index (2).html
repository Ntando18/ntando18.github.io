About N.T.D

Nontando Masuku
// Founder — N.T.D Data & Defense
The Story

N.T.D Data & Defense started as an impulsive thought — one of those ideas that arrives without warning and refuses to leave. What began as a passing "what if" quickly became something I could not ignore. I decided to take it seriously. I decided to take it further. I decided to build it.

At 20 years old, from KwaZulu-Natal, I built N.T.D from scratch — no office, no team, no funding. Just a laptop, a relentless drive to learn and an honest belief that small businesses across South Africa deserve access to professional data, cybersecurity and web development services without paying agency prices.

What started as an impulsive thought is now a real business — with real clients, real packages and a real mission to protect and empower businesses and communities across KZN and beyond. N.T.D is not just a business. It is proof that the right idea, pursued with enough conviction, becomes something extraordinary.

// Giving Back

Community Outreach

N.T.D Data & Defense believes that technology should protect and empower everyone — especially the next generation. That is why we invest in our communities beyond our business services.

🏫

School Outreach Programme

We visit high schools across Newcastle and KwaZulu-Natal to deliver free cybersecurity and digital safety awareness sessions to learners from Grade 8 to Grade 12.

👶

Early Childhood Digital Safety

We work with creches and primary schools to introduce young children to the basics of online safety — because catching them early makes all the difference.

💙

Mental Health & Cyberbullying Awareness

Cyberbullying is a mental health crisis affecting thousands of South African learners. We create safe spaces for young people to learn, ask questions and understand their rights online.

⚖️

Know Your Rights Online

Many learners do not know that cyberbullying is a criminal offence under South Africa's Cybercrimes Act of 2021. We teach them their rights and how to report abuse.

💬 Book a Free Session
// High Schools
Cybersecurity & Cyberbullying Awareness

A 45-minute to 1-hour interactive session covering what cybersecurity is, how to recognise cyberbullying, online safety tips, mental health impacts of social media and how to report digital abuse in South Africa.

100% FREE

Online Safety for Young Learners

A fun, age-appropriate session introducing young children to the basics of staying safe online — covering what information to keep private, how to recognise danger and what to tell a trusted adult.
